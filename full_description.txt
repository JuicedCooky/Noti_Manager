Organize your Android notifications into custom groups — and keep your notification shade clean.

HOW IT WORKS
Noti Manager runs a background notification listener that intercepts incoming notifications and re-posts them under your custom-defined groups. Assign apps to a group, and whenever one of those apps sends a notification, it gets bundled under that group in your notification shade.

INTERACTIVE BUBBLE CANVAS
The main screen is a zoomable, pannable canvas where each group appears as a cluster of bubbles. The center dot is the group; surrounding bubbles are its assigned apps. Drag groups anywhere on the canvas, zoom in and out, and rearrange to your liking.

- Drag an app bubble into another group to reassign it instantly
- Long-press an app bubble to open that app's system settings
- Long-press a group's center dot to edit it

GROUP CUSTOMIZATION
Each group can have a custom name, description, icon, dot color, and dot size. A per-group toggle lets you disable grouping for a specific group without deleting it, so its notifications pass through unchanged. A heads-up toggle controls whether grouped notifications pop up on screen.

SMART NOTIFICATION HANDLING
When a notification is grouped, the original is cancelled and re-posted as a child under a group summary with a custom header and colored avatar. Tapping a grouped notification still opens the correct app or action.

A global filter option lets you exclude ongoing and media notifications from being managed — so music players and navigation apps are never interrupted.

AUTOMATIC APP DISCOVERY
On first launch, all installed apps are placed into a default group. New apps installed later are added automatically. From there, drag them into the right group at your own pace.

GLOBAL ON/OFF SWITCH
A toggle at the top of the canvas disables notification management entirely, cancelling all managed notifications so everything flows through normally.

PRIVACY
All settings and group data are stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to any server.
